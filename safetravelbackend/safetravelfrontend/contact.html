About

A web platform that merges travel planning with disaster awareness to keep travelers informed and safe with real‑time alerts and safer routing.

Motivation

Travel apps optimize for convenience, while alert apps lack travel context. Combining them bridges a clear gap for safety‑centric trips.

Tech Stack

  • Backend: Spring Boot, MySQL
  • Maps: Google Maps Platform
