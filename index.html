Travel Smarter & Safer

Travel Tips During Disasters
  • Carry an emergency kit including water, snacks, and first aid.
  • Avoid low-lying areas prone to floods.
  • Check weather & road conditions before starting your journey.
  • Prefer safer routes over shorter ones if conditions are risky.
